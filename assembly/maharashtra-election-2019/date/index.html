Maharashtra Assembly election date 2019

Chief Election Commissioner (CEC) Sunil Arora announced in Delhi, Maharashtra legislative assembly election 2019 on Saturday (21.09.2019). Legislative Assembly election 2019 is planned to be held on 21 October 2019, Monday to elect the 288 members of Legislative Assembly. The results will be announced on 24 October 2019, Thursday.
