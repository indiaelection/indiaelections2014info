Suave and to-the-point, Captain Abhimanyu has been able to leave his mark as a national spokesperson of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) in a short span of time. His grasp over the subject, method of articulation and grip over language has endeared him not only to the media, but also have helped him earn accolades as a great debater too.

An organiser par excellence, the BJP leadership tasked him with managing party affairs in Uttar Pradesh as a co-in charge of the state along with with Sh. Amit Shah as incharge, which gave a stunning victory to the BJP in the 2014 Lok Sabha poll. Having risen from the level of grassroots, Captain Abhimanyu steered effectively the grand victory for the vision of Narendra Modi for the Lok Sabha poll, as a member of his core campaign team.

Born in 1967 in Khanda Kheri village of Hisar district in Haryana, Captain Abhimanyu hails from the family of great freedom fighters. His father late Shri Mitra Sen Arya is known for his participation in ‘Hindi Andolan’, a movement leading to creation of Haryana as a separate state. He was a leading fight of Arya Samaj in the country, a philanthropist and a patron of Gurukul system of education. Captain Abhimanyu has carried forward his father’s precious legacy by actively engaging himself in activities aimed at building a vibrant Haryana.

Academics: Captain Abhimanyu did his Bachelor of Commerce from the Maharishi Dayanand University, Rothak, in 1986. He is LLB from the MDU. He did his Post Graduate Diploma in Mass Communication from the Guru Jamabheshwar University, Hisar in 2007 and is now pursuing the Owner President Management Program, a three year part time executive management program from the Harvard business school, at Harvard University. Responding to his inner urge to dedicate his life for bigger public cause, he gave up his successfully running business and chose to work as an active BJP worker. In 2002, he had been conferred with the highly acclaimed Babu Baal Mukund Gupt award for Journalism by Haryana Sahitya Academi.
