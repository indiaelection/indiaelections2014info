A life dedicated to public service: With almost forty years of active and dedicated public service to the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh and Bhartiya Janata Party, Manohar Lal Khattar has worked tirelessly for both Haryana and the country. Intimately and inseparably involved with his ‘Karmabhoomi’ Haryana for years, Manohar Lal’s vision for Haryana is founded on his active involvement in the state’s politics and people.

Recognised for his political acumen, Manohar Lal has played the role of a master strategist and successfully led many electoral campaigns, the latest being the 2014 Parliamentary elections in Haryana as Chairman of Election Committee. He has been selflessly working behind the scenes in key party positions that have earned him the reputation of a tough task master with a no-nonsense approach and proven organisational skills. He has stayed at the forefront of innumerable social projects and humanitarian efforts. A man who believes in keeping up with the times, he is at ease with today’s ever changing technology – using it to his advantage for effective communication with the outside world..

Choosing to remain a bachelor, Manohar Lal opted to sacrifice his personal life and dedicated himself to public service. His image is pristine, untarnished by scandals and charges, instead holding the idea of selfless service to the nation above personal agenda and gratification.

Close to his roots and a believer in the power of culture and tradition, politics for Manohar Lal is an instrument to bring change and improve lives of citizens today. A leader, he feels, has to have vision and should be able to motivate people to bring about change at the same time. Holding the sobriquet of a ‘Swayam Sevak’ close to his heart, Manohar Lal has always willingly accepted any responsibility entrusted on him by his party.

Manohar Lal’s agenda promises not only a better Haryana, but a unique Haryana that leads in various fields – acknowledged not only nationally but internationally and set as an example for others states to follow.
