Om Prakash Dhankhar/ O P Dhankhar (born - 1961) is an Indian politician and social activist, belonging to the Bhartiya Janata Party (BJP). Sh O P Dhankhar is Cabinet Minister for Agriculture and allied portfolios in the Government of Haryana. In the 2014 Lok Sabha election, he contested as the BJP candidate from the Rohtak constituency and gave a tough competition by finishing second, with 319436 votes (31%%).

He is national coordinator for the prestigious Mr Narender Modi's 'Statue of Unity' project and has been twice elected as BJP's Kisan Morcha President.

Academics & Early Life: Shri Om Prakash Dhankhar did his elementary education from the village school before earning his Masters & M. Ed. from the Maharishi Dayanand University, Rohtak. Post qualification, he served as a Geography lecturer at Bhivani for 11 years. As an academician, he was well respected for his insightful views on education reforms and issues concerning student lives.
