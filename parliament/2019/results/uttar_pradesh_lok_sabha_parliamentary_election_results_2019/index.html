Uttar Pradesh lok sabha election results 2019

The 2019 Indian general (lok sabha) election in Uttar Pradesh was held on seven phases from 11 April to 19 May 2019 to constitute the 17th Lok Sabha. Counting was held on 23 May 2019 and result was also declared on same day.
