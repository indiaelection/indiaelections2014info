Madhya Pradesh election 2019 date

The India General election 2019 will be held in seven phase from 11 April 2019 and the results will be announced on May 23 (Thursday), to constitute the 17th Lok Sabha. Voting will be held in the 29 Lok Sabha (parliamentary) constituency in Madhya Pradesh on April 29, May 6, May 12 and May 19.
