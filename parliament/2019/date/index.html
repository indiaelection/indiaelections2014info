The General election will be held in seven phase from 11 April 2019 and the results will be announced on May 23 (Thursday), to constitute the 17th Lok Sabha. Voting will be held in India on April 11, April 19, April 23, April 29, May 6, May 12 and May 19. Legislative Assembly elections in the states of Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Haryana, Odisha, Sikkim and Jammu and Kashmir are expected to be held simultaneously with the general elections.
