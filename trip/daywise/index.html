The Indian Motorcycle Diary 2015 route map

Starting point and date:- Sampranikodi (30.05.2015), in Thrikkaruva Village of Kollam District, Kerala State and covering all Villages and Panchayaths in Half of Kollam District, then to the rest of districts of Kerala and ending the First part of Kerala at Talappady, Kerala Near Karnataka border. Then continuing the expedition in the state wise order of Karnataka, Goa, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Rajasthan, Punjab, Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh,

Uttarakhand, Haryana, Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, through West Bengal to Sikkim, again through West Bengal to Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram, Tripura, through Assam to Meghalaya, remaining parts of Assam to West Bengal, Jharkhand, Odisha, through Chhattisgarh to Madhya Pradesh, back to remaining parts Chhattisgarh, to Telengana to Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, back to remaining parts of Kerala and at the end to the starting place of this journey.
