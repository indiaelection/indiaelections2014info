The Indian Motorcycle Diary 2015

Round the country on mobike in 120 days

KOLLAM (30.05.2015): A youth from Kollam is all set to travel 175,000 km across the country on his 100 cc motorbike, covering 29 states and six Union Territories, to break into the Guinness World Records. The ride, named ‘India on two wheels’, begins on Saturday and will cover all the taluks in the country in 120 days. Read more in Deccan Chronicle

The aim of Indian Motorcycle Diary 2015

The motivation of Indian Motorcycle Diary 2015 is to spread the vision and message of Unity & Integrity to all over India. “ We differ in our Languages, We differ in our Culture, We differ in our Tradition, We differ in our Lifestyle, We differ in our Habits, but WE are ONE, ONE INDIA and We love our country more than anything else.” “ All for ONE and ONE for ALL ” A Bharath Yatra, (An India Tour) to set a new World Records, travelling an estimated distance of 1,50,000+ KMs covering 29 States & 5 Union Territories, 541 Loksabha Constituencies, 588 District Panchayaths , 672 Districts, 4215 Legislative Assembly Constituencies, 5,553 Tehsils (Taluk) , 6,312 Block Panchayaths, 2,53,110 Grama (Village) Panchayaths, 6,48,894 Villages, 15,00,000+ Schools & Colleges.. Etc.
